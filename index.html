About Us

Studio FX 8


Studio8fx LLC. is a post-production company located in Los Angeles, California that supports work for visual effects in 2D and 3D. Studio8fx provides services such as roto, wire & rig removal, 3d Tracking, match mover, as well as dirt & scratch removal.

Our business model is to serve as a resource for all post-production companies. Currently, with margins being squeezed, direct costs have a large impact on the bottom line. By using our reliable and cost-effective services you can focus on the art and craft of visual effects while increasing your profit margins.

Studio8 was founded in California by a group of experienced local industry executives who recognize the world is changing and are able to offer flexibility in the global market which is essential to meet the challenges of the future.
